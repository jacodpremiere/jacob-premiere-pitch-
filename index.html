Introduction

Jacob Premiere is a secure, direct-to-home cinematic experience platform. It offers blockbuster premieres, immersive interactivity, and a new revenue model for filmmakers—combining high-end technology with emotional engagement and blockchain-based monetization.

What is Jacob Premiere?

Jacob Premiere is a premium digital platform that redefines home entertainment by bringing the cinema experience directly to you.

  • 🎬 Streams first-run movies directly to Smart TVs.

  • 🤝 Shares up to 80% of revenue with creators.

Real-Time Multi-Language Dubbing - A Global Breakthrough

Jacob Premiere integrates cutting-edge AI-powered dubbing technology, allowing users to experience movie premieres in any language instantly — with voiceovers generated in real time that match the emotion, tone, and timing of the original performance.

Key Features:

  • Instant dubbing in over 40+ languages.
  • AI-generated voices trained to sound natural, expressive, and culturally adapted.
  • Lip-sync matching with real-time dialogue rendering.
  • Automatic language detection based on user location or preference.
  • Option to switch between original audio, dubbed voice, or subtitles with a single click.

Strategic Impact:

  • Expands global reach without waiting for post-production translation.
  • Empowers inclusivity by offering access in native language at launch.
  • Boosts engagement & immersion, especially for non-English speaking households.

Feel The Hero - Interactive Experience

"Live the Emotion. Own the Story." Feel The Hero is our exclusive feature that deepens viewer-character bonds, transforming passive watching into an immersive, real-time interactive event.

Imagine watching your favorite character scream, fight, or fall in love - and your smart-connected collectible vibrates, lights up, or plays audio synced to the scene. This real-time feedback creates deep viewer-character bonds.

Monetization & Formats:

  • Sell physical/digital collectibles ($5 – $60 USD).
  • Monthly subscription for interactive premium content.
  • Integrated with Axinom DRM for security, functions only during active playback on Jacob Premiere.
