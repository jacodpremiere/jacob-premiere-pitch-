Unmatched Security with Axinom DRM

Because true cinematic excellence demands unbreakable protection.

Jacob Premiere ensures that every film premiere is delivered with the highest level of protection through Axinom's enterprise-grade Digital Rights Management (DRM) and Forensic Watermarking technology. This isn't just security, it's industry-leading peace of mind.

Key Security Advantages:

  • End-to-End Encryption: All content is encrypted and streamed exclusively to authorized devices.
  • Device Fingerprinting: Each viewing session is tied to a unique user and device, making leaks traceable.
  • Invisible Watermarking: Undetectable forensic watermarks are embedded in every stream to identify unauthorized copies.
  • Playback Authorization: Only users who have completed NDA verification can unlock and access protected content.
